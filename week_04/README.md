# Iowa Liquor Sales Analysis – Pernod Ricard

This project analyzes the Iowa Liquor Sales database with a focus on the vendor **Pernod Ricard USA/Austin Nichols**. The goal is to understand sales performance, product demand, store performance, geographic sales patterns, and potential market opportunities.

The analysis uses SQL queries to explore transaction-level sales data, product details, store information, and county population data. Key techniques include filtering, aggregation, joins, `HAVING` clauses, and subqueries.

Main areas analyzed include:

- Total sales revenue and average bottle price
- Most frequently purchased Pernod Ricard products
- Highest and lowest performing stores
- Revenue by county and store status
- Mid-tier price bracket performance
- Category comparison against competitor product groups
- Untapped markets based on county population and zero sales
- Stores performing above average revenue

This project helps identify Pernod Ricard’s strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats within the Iowa liquor market using data-driven SQL analysis.
